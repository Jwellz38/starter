Starter

Get off and running with Starter. A light weight project starter for building web applications. A way to make life a little easier for web developers and designers. Starter comes equiped with gulpjs, bootstrap, jquery, browsersync auto-reload, and sass compilation included and configured out of the box. So in just a few clicks your ready to go. After downloading, all you have to is right click in the root of the starter folder and open with your favorite CLI. Then type gulp serve. That's it! Of course, you can extend it as much as you need to.
